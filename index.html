About us

In a changing environment and in growing and irreversible globalization,Lima chamber of commerce today the has one of the most modern business complexes in the country and has aligned itself with cutting-edge technology platforms, in line with the requirements of competitiveness and the internationalisation of trade. As "The Chamber of Guilds" the Lima chamber of commerce has more than 15,000 registered companies from commerce, production and services, representing 41.1% of the total exported value and 52.3% of the total imported value, having planned to count on the commemoration of the National Bicentennial in 2021, with 20,000 associated companies.
